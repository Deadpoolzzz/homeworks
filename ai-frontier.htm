SCHEDULE

Santa Clara Convention. Jan 11, 2017

Keynote:

Overview of Current Frontiers of Artificial Intelligence

Morning Session 1. Autonomous Driving

We are bringing a panel of industry experts from companies that are actively developing autonomous cars. Our speakers include Zhaoyin Jia, tech lead of Google Self Driving, Junli Gu, tech lead of Machine Learning of Tesla Autopilot, and Tony Han, Director of Baidu Autonomous Driving Unit. 

*Each panelist will give a presentation for 15 minutes, followed by 30-minute panel discussion, moderated by the session chair. *

Morning Session 2. Speech-enabled Personal Assistants 

We are bringing a panel of industry experts and leading scientists to discuss Speech-enabled Personal Assistants. Our speakers include Li Deng, Chief Scientist of AI of Microsoft, Adam Coates, Director of AI lab of Baidu, and more.     

*Each panelist will give a presentation for 15 minutes, followed by 30-minute panel discussion, moderated by the session chair. * 

Morning Session 3. Natural Language Processing

We are bringing a panel of industry experts and leading scientists to discuss Natural Language Processing products, including Machine Translation, Automatic Email Response, and Question Answering Systems. Our speakers include Richard Socher, Chief Scientist of Salesforce, Lukasz Kaiser of Google Brain team, and more.

*Each panelist will give a presentation for 15 minutes, followed by 30-minute panel discussion, moderated by the session chair. *

Lunch

Afternoon Session 1. IoT (Internet of Things) 

We are bringing a panel of industry experts from companies that are actively developing Connected Home and Building Technology. Our speakers include Sean Ding, Chief Scientist from Huawei, Mohak Shah, Head of Data Science from Bosch, and more.  

*Each panelist will give a presentation for 15 minutes, followed by 30-minute panel discussion, moderated by the session chair. *

Afternoon Session 2. Computer Vision

We are bringing a panel of industry experts and leading scientists to discuss the newest development in computer vision, including photo and video processing. Our speakers include Gary Bradski, Founder of OpenCV, Liu Ren, Chief Scientist of Bosch, and Shinko Cheng of Google Photo. 

*Each panelist will give a presentation for 15 minutes, followed by 30-minute panel discussion, moderated by the session chair. *

Afternoon Session 3. Deep Learning Frameworks

We are bringing together leaders of major deep learning frameworks including TensorFlow, Torch and more. Our speakers will give overview of their framework, and discuss the advantage of adopting their specific framework. Our speaker include Rajat Monga, Leader of TensorFlow and Engineering Director of Google, maintainer of Torch project, and more. 

*Each panelist will give a presentation for 15 minutes, followed by 30-minute panel discussion, moderated by the session chair. *

 

Afternoon Keynote
